Little Menagerie

“Little Menagerie” is a virtual reality experience that explores the concept of world building, literally. The player acts as a god-like entity looking down upon a small environment and has the ability to both create and destroy as they please. To further emphasize this point, the setting is themed around papercraft. Everything in the world is styled towards a light, cutesy tone and designed with a low-poly aesthetic in line with folded paper. Buildings, trees, as well as the people, are all heavily inspired by paper folding crafts, even to the point of their fragility. If the player interacts with any of the objects too roughly, they would crumple or flatten.
