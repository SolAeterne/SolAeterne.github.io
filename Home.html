An exploration on the question of, “In a 3D video game, could narrative interpretation be changed by altering the techniques used to build the world?” By experimenting with the effects of adapting the famous fairy tale Little Red Riding Hood with popular genre styles such as Film Noir, this project seeks to better understand the power that world building has over a players understanding of a narrative in 3D games.
