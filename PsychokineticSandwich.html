Psychokinetic Sandwich

“Psychokinetic Sandwich” is a virtual reality experience designed for the Google Cardboard. The player equipped with the ability to move things with their mind, but only sandwich related items. Not only that, the player can materialize objects out of thin air. Again, only sandwich related items. Simply look at the desired ingredient and a slice will appear ready for sandwich making needs. Place the ingredients in the assembly area and build the best sandwich ever made with psychokinesis.

This project was built in the Unity game engine in tangent with the Google Cardboard SDK. The intention for this project was for it to be played only with the phone without the use of another controller and while the magnetic switch was an option, the idea of controlling purely with the head through the use of raycasting was a fairly amusing concept. To enhance the feeling of psychokinesis, the conjured objects follow the sight line of the player as though it was being pulled in low gravity.
