Dessert Island

Dessert island is virtual reality experience that allows the viewer to wander through a paradise island themed entirely around desserts. The island completely made up of sweets, both flora and fauna are made from sugary snacks. Everything from cake mountains and candy trees to a cupcake hamlet complete with anthropomorphized dessert folk.

I was tasked with creating various natural and urban environmental set pieces that were dotted around the island. Some simply meant finding suitable replacements that seemed fitting. Cakes make lovely mountains, lollipops and suckers made for nice smaller trees, and a lava cake makes an excellent volcano centerpiece. The village assets required a slightly different approach. For buildings, identifiable features like doorways turned assorted cupcakes into simple houses. For more complicated structures multiple treats acted as components that made up the final model. Chocolate benches with biscuit struts, candy cane streetlights lit with gumdrop bulbs, and a chocolate fountain fitted with a donut base, it is a sugary sweet town square.
