Sunny is a student at OCAD University studying in the Digital Futures program. He strives to explore the ins and outs of world building as well as story telling with an accompanying medium of 3D modelling and video games.

At OCAD University, he crafted stories and experimented with various mediums to tell said tales, with a tendency to lean towards time based media and single channel work, all of which is digital. He has a wide variety of skills under his belt including filming, compositing, and editing, but has begun to build a passion for 3D modelling and animation within the field of gaming. Some of his works employ the use of the Unity engine and even broach the field of AR, VR, and the creation of digital games. As well, he enjoys the exploration of world building and working with video games as a medium for storytelling. Though his works are not all confined to one unified theme, many are set within their own world and tell their own story.

He aspires to strengthen the skills that he has in 3D modelling and animation to better construct the worlds that of his imagination. Moreover, he strives to reinforce his storytelling and world building capabilities to further enhance what he can create.
