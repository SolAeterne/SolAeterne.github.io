Augmented Museum

An augmented reality project that takes artworks created by other artists that were hung in the OCAD building, and augments them with 3D animated models viewable through a phone. This project explored the possibilities of AR in enhancing the viewing experience of artwork.

The 3D models, created in 3DS Max, were made based upon the original artworks themselves mixed with my own interpretation of the piece.

The augmented reality side of the project was done using the Unity game engine paired with the Vuforia SDK. The artworks were used as the AR image target, both because of their unique and complex design suitable for being the target, as well as for the ease in the positioning of the 3D models. With that in mind, for permanently stationary objects like museum art, a separate target could be placed off to the side for better viewing purposes.
